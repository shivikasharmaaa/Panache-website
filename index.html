About Us

The power of words is often overlooked by the masses. We at Panache strive to develop a community of students who not only understand this power but also use it to the best of their abilities. We aim to help students sharpen their oratory skills by conducting various competitions, workshops, and events. Oratory skill improves communication skills with others and it also increases skill at perceiving people's reactions to your message. It helps to make them more confident around people automatically and makes them more comfortable around other people. It has now become a vital skill for every person regardless of their age, gender and background. The object of oratory alone in not truth, but persuasion.” Panache, the oratory club of IIT Goa, aims at enhancing the student’s ability to communicate their feelings by understanding their own emotions. The club provides a platform to the orators to converge and hone their debating, leadership and public speaking skills.

Events

Quizzaholic Bandits

December, 2021

This is a quizzing competition. It helps in identifying the students what they know and what they don't. It increases the level of practice, and concentration and increases the uplift of the memorizing level at its best.

Debatomania

December, 2021

Students debate on topics that compel them to think of whys and hows on real-world issues while practicing and competing on their ability to present their case using just the right words. The format of the debate can vary from a parliamentary debate to a simple debate where each person is given 3 minutes to speak followed by a rebuttal round.

Out of The Box

December, 2021

This was a case study competition held in collaboration with the Entrepreneurship Club of IIT Goa. Here, students present their case studies in the field of engineering, finance, entrepreneurship, management, and science.

TV Trivia

December, 2021

This is a fun event where student teams answer questions based on TV shows and movies.

Just a Minute

December, 2021

JAMs, typically conducted every Saturday, as the name suggests, students are supposed to speak on the spot for a minute on a topic that has just been introduced to them.

Gumnaam

December, 2021

In this competition, the students are given a picture and they have to come up with the most innovative meaning that the picture contains in a brief writeup for the same.
