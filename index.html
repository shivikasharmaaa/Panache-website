PANACHE
The object of oratory alone in not truth, but persuasion.” Panache, the oratory club of IIT Goa, aims at enhancing the student’s ability to communicate their feelings by understanding their own emotions. The club provides a platform to the orators to converge and hone their debating, leadership and public speaking skills.
